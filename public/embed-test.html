Auto-Injection Test

Top Notice Feature

You can trigger a top notice by adding attributes to the banner tag:

<syndorela-banner top-notice="1" notice-duration="15"></syndorela-banner>

How to embed

Simply include the script and use the tag:

<script src="https://syndorela.com/embed/syndorela-banner.js"></script>
<syndorela-banner></syndorela-banner>
